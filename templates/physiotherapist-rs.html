physiotherapist Resources

A physiotherapist is a healthcare professional dedicated to helping people restore, maintain, and improve their physical movement and overall well-being. They assess, diagnose, and treat a wide range of physical conditions, from sports injuries and post-surgery rehabilitation to chronic pain and mobility issues. Through personalized exercise programs, manual therapy, and patient education, physiotherapists empower individuals to regain strength, reduce pain, and achieve a healthier, more active lifestyle
