Blockchain Engineering Project Ideas

1. Cryptocurrency Wallet (Beginner Level)

A simple application that allows users to create wallets, send and receive tokens, and view their balance.

Technologies to Use:

JavaScript/TypeScript, Node.js, Express.js, MongoDB, Web3.js or Ethers.js.

Key Learning:

Understanding blockchain basics, wallet creation, and transaction handling.

2. Decentralized Voting System (Intermediate Level)

A secure blockchain-based voting platform ensuring transparency, tamper-proof records, and anonymity.

Technologies to Use:

Solidity (for smart contracts), Ethereum, Hardhat/Truffle, React.js, IPFS.

Key Learning:

Smart contract development, blockchain-based record storage, and DApp integration.

3. Supply Chain Management System (Advanced Level)

A blockchain-powered platform that tracks product movement from manufacturer to consumer, ensuring transparency and authenticity.

Technologies to Use:

Hyperledger Fabric or Ethereum, Solidity/Chaincode, Node.js, React.js, IPFS, Docker.

Key Learning:

Complex smart contract development, enterprise-level blockchain frameworks, and integration with real-world business processes.
