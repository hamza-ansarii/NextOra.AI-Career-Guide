This practice focuses on understanding comedic timing, punchline delivery, and stage presence. It helps beginners gain confidence in speaking and making simple jokes land effectively.

  • Using mirror practice for expressions and gestures
  • Building confidence through small storytelling sessions

At this stage, comedians learn to expand from one-liners to longer stories, improvise with audience interactions, and develop unique comedic styles.

  • Experimenting with different comedic styles (observational, satire, character-based)
  • Developing natural body language and stage presence
  • Testing material at open mics or small group settings
